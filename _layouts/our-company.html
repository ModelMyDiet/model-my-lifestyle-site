Our approach

What modeling your lifestyle is all about

Unlike some other weight loss website Model My Lifestyle is focused on encouraging and promoting a healthy lifestyle, not a diet culture community. We have the knowledge to help you lose weight but the right way. All you need to bring to the tablets a positive mindset, hard work and consistency. We're here to motivate and inspire you with our unique body visualizer simulator, as well as our website full of articles on all things health and fitness, our own healthy recipes, and our excellent meal plans and workout regimes.

We're all about creating an inclusive fit community. Were passionate about helping you achieve your health and fitness goals. but we're also here to remind you that you're amazing as you are. We believe in loving your body and becoming the best version of you that you can be. rather than setting unattainable goals to look like someone else. Some weight loss blogs may focus on how to lose weight as quickly as possible. but we're here to help you get fit and healthy. and STAY fit and healthy - we're In it for the long run!

It doesn't matter what stage of fitness you are at, or what your goal weight is. as with our awesome team and our body visualizer, we'll help you achieve your goal and then improve on it even further! Think of us as your own personal trainer and cheerleader. where other weight loss websites simply show you how to do exercises. were also here to keep you positive and motivated! We're not about bland, boring diet foods here! We love health, nutritious food, and we're keen to help you feel the same way about healthy food too!
